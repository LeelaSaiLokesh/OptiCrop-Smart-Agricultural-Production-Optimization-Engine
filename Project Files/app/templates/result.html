Expert Guidance

Farming Best Practices

Professional agronomic guidance for maximizing yield and crop quality.

🌱

Seed Selection

  • Use certified high-yielding varieties (HYV)
  • Prefer disease-resistant, region-adapted varieties
  • Treat seeds with bio-fungicide before sowing
  • Ensure germination rate ≥ 85% before use
🌍

Soil Preparation

  • Deep plough to 20–25 cm before planting
  • Incorporate 8–12 tonnes FYM or compost per hectare
  • Test soil pH and correct before sowing
  • Ensure good drainage and aeration
💧

Irrigation

  • Follow recommended irrigation method for this crop
  • Irrigate at critical growth stages (flowering, grain fill)
  • Use drip or sprinkler to improve water-use efficiency
  • Stop irrigation 10–14 days before harvest
🧪

Fertilizer Usage

  • Apply recommended NPK doses per soil test
  • Split nitrogen into 3 doses for improved NUE
  • Apply micronutrients (Zn, B) if deficient
  • Avoid excessive N — promotes vegetative growth over yield
🌿

Weed Management

  • Weed at 20–25 and 40–45 days after sowing
  • Apply pre-emergence herbicide at recommended dose
  • Use inter-row cultivation tools
  • Maintain crop canopy to shade and suppress weeds
🐛

Pest & Disease Control

  • Conduct regular scouting from early growth stage
  • Use pheromone traps for key pest monitoring
  • Apply IPM-approved pesticides at threshold levels
  • Release bio-control agents where available
🌾

Harvest Tips

  • Harvest at physiological maturity for best quality
  • Use appropriate machinery to reduce field losses
  • Harvest in dry weather to prevent post-harvest loss
  • Monitor grain moisture at cutting time
🏪

Post-harvest Storage

  • Dry produce to safe moisture level before storage
  • Store in hermetic bags or silos to prevent pests
  • Use appropriate fumigation for long-term storage
  • Register with nearest APMC for MSP procurement
